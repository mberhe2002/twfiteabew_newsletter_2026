May the grace of the Holy Trinity be upon you. We sincerely thank everyone who participated in our fundraising program on March 21, 2026, and all those who continue to support Twfite Abew through prayer, encouragement, and generous giving.

Your support sustains Felege Hiwot students, teachers, clergy, and communities across Tigray. It helps provide spiritual formation, education, meals, learning materials, and the practical assistance needed for faithful service to continue.

Your support is more than a contribution—it is a blessing that strengthens the Church’s mission, brings hope to many hearts, and helps communities move toward spiritual, social, and emotional healing.

Why This Work Remains Urgent

Communities in Tigray continue to face a shortage of trained Church servants and Gospel teachers, limited access to religious education, the closure and destruction of monasteries and churches, displacement of priests and students, growing secular influence, and serious psychological and social hardship.

Twfite Abew responds by strengthening Felege Hiwot centers, preparing Gospel teachers and preachers, supporting ministry in both rural and urban communities, and promoting healing, safety, and hope through spiritual service.

What Your Support Has Made Possible

Through your generosity, assistance has included:

  • Dining facilities and monthly food support
  • Specialized spiritual and educational training
  • Graduation assistance for students
  • Bibles and other learning materials
  • Ongoing support for Gospel teachers and Church servants

During 2025, a five-year strategic plan was developed, a full-time servant was appointed to monitor educational progress and provide training, and specialized instruction was delivered across the supported Felege Hiwot centers. Monthly support for students and teachers was also increased.

Additional assistance supported graduates of the Axum Tsion Gubae Bet, a teacher serving through May Tselot, Sunday School, and rural outreach, as well as a teacher facing a serious health challenge. Training topics included apologetics, apostolic mission, patrology, salvation and healing, pastoral ministry, priesthood, discipleship, and religious education.

In Loving Memory of ክብርት መጋቢት ምስጢር መምህርት ሕይወት ፀሐየ

We are deeply saddened to share that one of the devoted teachers who served the Church and helped form students in the faith has fallen asleep in the Lord. Her life of prayer, learning, teaching, and service touched many students, disciples, and fellow servants.

A memorial program is being prepared at Axum Tsion Hall for Ginbot 23, 2018 E.C. The commemoration will include Gospel and funeral prayers, hymns, qene, a sermon, reflections from her disciples and invited guests, and the lighting of candles in her memory. A commemorative publication is also being prepared to preserve the story of her childhood, education, monastic journey, teaching ministry, qene, writings, and faithful witness.

We ask you to remember her soul, her family, her students, and the entire community in your prayers. May God grant her rest among the righteous and comfort all who mourn her passing.
